# KonekTravay 🌍

> *"Konekte ak travay ki fèt pou ou"* — Connect with work made for you

---

## The Problem

Haiti has one of the highest youth unemployment rates in the Western Hemisphere. Millions of young Haitians are skilled, multilingual, and motivated — yet systematically excluded from the global remote work economy.

The barrier is not talent. It is access, presentation, and safety.

Without a resume, without guidance, and without protection from the scams that target vulnerable job seekers, talented Haitian youth have no pathway in. KonekTravay builds that pathway.

---

## The Solution

KonekTravay is an AI-powered economic mobility platform that connects Haitian youth to legitimate remote work opportunities worldwide.

A user describes what they know how to do — in Haitian Creole, French, or English, in their own words — and KonekTravay does the rest. It builds their professional profile, writes their resume, matches them to vetted remote jobs, detects scams, coaches them through interviews, and tracks their applications — all accessible directly over WhatsApp with no laptop or app download required.

AI is not a feature of KonekTravay. AI is the product. Every core function — skill extraction, resume generation, job matching, scam detection, interview coaching — is powered by artificial intelligence.

---

## Why Haiti, Why Now

The global remote work economy represents a generational opportunity for Haitian youth. The tools that make remote work accessible have never been designed for them — until now.

KonekTravay is built for the real constraints of life in Haiti: intermittent internet, Android phones, limited data, and three languages spoken in the same household. It is lightweight, offline-capable, and works on any connection.

Every placement is an act of economic justice. Every profile created is a door opened.

---

## Target Market

**Primary:** Haitian youth aged 18–35 with informal or self-taught skills and no formal work history, located across Haiti and in the diaspora.

**Delivery channels:**
- WhatsApp Bot — reaches users with no smartphone app or browser required
- Progressive Web App (PWA) — installable on any Android device
- Community partnerships with NGOs, churches, and youth organizations across Haiti

**Market size:** 4.5 million youth in Haiti. Tens of thousands in the diaspora actively seeking income opportunities.

---

## Validation

- 3 pilot personas tested end-to-end with real skill inputs, generating accurate profiles, resumes, and job matches
- WhatsApp bot flow validated across all three languages (Creole, French, English)
- Job dataset of 25 vetted remote positions curated and scam-scored
- Scam detection system tested against real fraudulent job postings circulating in Haiti
- Prototype publicly available and functional at the time of submission

## Unique Advantage

- **Cultural ownership:** Built by a Haitian, for Haitians — not adapted from a Western product
- **WhatsApp-first:** The only remote work platform in Haiti that works entirely over WhatsApp, meeting users on the channel they already use daily
- **Trilingual AI:** Skill extraction and coaching natively supports Haitian Creole — a capability no mainstream platform offers
- **Scam protection:** Active fraud detection built into the core flow, directly addressing the most cited fear of Haitian job seekers
- **Low-bandwidth by design:** ~150KB per session — purpose-built for Haitian internet realities
